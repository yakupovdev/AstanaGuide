Local Events & Sights Guide

Astana is a city where futuristic skyscrapers meet rich cultural traditions. Here you can explore unique architecture like Bayterek Tower and Khan Shatyr, enjoy local festivals, concerts, and sports events. This guide will help you find the best places to see and the most exciting things to do in the capital of Kazakhstan.
