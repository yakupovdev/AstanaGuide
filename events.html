Popular Events in Astana

Meirambek Besbayev & Abdizhappar Alkozha

📅 November 6, 2025 — 19:30

Two iconic Kazakh singers will perform together in a special concert celebrating national music, culture, and unity. Expect emotional ballads and modern hits.

Aleksey Kvashonkin Stand-up

📅 October 29, 2025 — 19:30

A rising Russian stand-up comedian known for sharp humor and witty takes on everyday life. A night full of laughter and clever observations.

Kazakhstan vs Liechtenstein

📅 October 10, 2025

A FIFA World Cup 2026 qualifying match where Kazakhstan fights for valuable points. The Astana Arena will be filled with energy and national pride. \

Kazakhstan vs Belgium

📅 October 13, 2025

An electrifying World Cup 2026 qualifier as Kazakhstan hosts one of the strongest European teams. Thousands of fans, world-class football, and unforgettable atmosphere.

The Rasmus — Live

📅 October 31, 2025

The legendary Finnish rock band famous for *“In the Shadows”* returns with a mix of old hits and new songs in their atmospheric style.

Andrea Vanzo — Piano Concert

📅 November 22, 2025

Italian pianist Andrea Vanzo presents a cinematic piano concert blending classical elegance with modern soundscapes.

“Kuzgi Auen” (Autumn Melodies) — Astana Opera

📅 Fall 2025

A concert by students from creative development centers of Astana — part of the “Damu Bala” programme. Expect choral and dance performances, soloists, and a diverse music selection including Boccherini, Tchaikovsky, Denza, Dunaevsky, Tariverdiev, Kunanbayev, and more. Family-friendly (5+).

“Schaherezada” Ballet — Astana Opera

📅 September 20, 2025 — 19:00

A major ballet performance of *Scheherazade*, known for its exotic storytelling and lush orchestration. The production features intricate set designs, virtuosic dancing, and vibrant costumes. Perfect for fans of classical ballet and dramatic stage works.

“Touch the Light” & “A Fuego…” — Astana Ballet

📅 November 1, 2025

An innovative ballet blending neoclassical dance with music, street art, fashion, and calligraphy. The show explores themes of inner light, the human soul, and the emotional journey of falling in love. A modern, visually rich, and emotionally charged performance.
